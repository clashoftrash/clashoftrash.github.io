What is COT?




The Why

Every year, trash and litter is cluttering our world more and more. Over 9 billion tons of litter enters the oceans annually, and over $11.5 billion dollars is invested into cleaning up litter. As trash makes its way into the environment, animals and plants can be harmed by getting caught in it.

We aim to reduce the impact of litter by incorporating social media games to encourage the pickup of trash. Through the form a game, we see an opportunity to prevent the destruction of the environment.

The How

The goal of Clash of Trash is to harnass the power of social media and gamification elements to make the incentive for people to do their part. We do this with a few techniques.

We use social media for its ability to shape peoples' behavior. People want to be recongnized and seek likes and follows. We can use this desire to help clean the world. To do this, we've made an Android App that runs off of a firebase database.
